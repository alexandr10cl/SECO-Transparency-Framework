About Us

A dedicated team from UNIRIO focused on inovations and tecnology

Our Group

Our group has been part of the Complex Systems Engineering Laboratory (LabESC) since January 2024, and our goal is to conduct scientific research in order to bring technological innovations.

We act as a bridge between students interested in research and faculty advisors, facilitating access to scientific initiation opportunities both within and outside the university.

Our group consists of two undergraduate students from the Information Systems program, one PhD student, and one Master's student, both in the field of technology.

Our Goals

Cientific Formation

To empower undergraduate students and researchers in scientific research methodologies and practices, preparing them for academic or professional careers.

Integration

To promote integration among students and researchers from different programs and educational levels, creating a multidisciplinary learning environment.

Research output

To increase UNIRIO's scientific output by supporting undergraduate research projects and publishing papers in conferences and journals.
